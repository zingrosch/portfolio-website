Écolo

Écolo turns shopping into a responsible action, letting users buy all kind of items while contributing to an ecologically sustainable world.

This online store helps users buy products while supporting sustainable economic concepts, protecting the environment and ensuring fair wages for everyone involved.

My Role

I was responsible for branding, research and User Interface Design, with deliverables including low-, mid-and high-fidelity wireframes.

Guiding Principles

Shopping should be easy for everyone, without harming the environment. Écolo provides a platform where goods can be bought online easily, at a fair price for everyone involved.

With a purchase in this store, you are not simply exchanging money for goods. You also support sustainable economic concepts, protect the environment and ensure fair wages.

What

A mobile app and a website for an online shop.

Who

Écolo is for people who don’t want to accept the way online shopping currently works with all its impact on the environment and society. Most of the users will be proficient with technology but will greatly benefit from the basic navigation and clean design.

When

This product will be used on commutes to and from work, in the evenings, during lunch breaks, and on weekends.

Where

Users can easily shop from anywhere they can connect to the internet, whether on a mobile device or on a desktop.

Why

Users don’t always have the time or ability to visit physical stores and would rather shop online.
