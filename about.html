About

My Story

I am a Content Specialist turned UI Designer with a passion for analogue photography and screenprinting. Those insights guide me when creating designs that speak to users and help them navigate interfaces with ease. I love getting new ideas, taking in different opinions and implementing them into my craft.

Did you know that people look at a website for only a few seconds before they decide if they like it or not? As a UI designer, I use data to generate insights and make informed decisions.

In my previous jobs, I learned a lot about user behaviour and found it so interesting, especially the psychology behind it, that I decided to go deeper into that topic.

My previous job I was working in a fast-paced environments which taught me how to remain flexible during times of change. When it comes to project management I am solution-focused and good at bringing structure into chaotic situations.

Due to my work experience in several countries, my cross-cultural communication skills are outstanding. I am good at reaching solutions among departments to meet the needs of business partners and customers alike. I would love to work for businesses providing an inclusive environment where I can grow, be it as a designer or in another area within UI such as user psychology or accessibility.
